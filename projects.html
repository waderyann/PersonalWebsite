Projects

Game Design & Software Dev

These are projects that I've worked on in the past, with students, teachers and companies within the last few years.

    PROXEMICS

    Proximal Spaces’ is a multi-modal exhibition that explores the environment at multiple scales in concentric circles of proximity to the body.
    Inspired by Edward Hall’s 1961 notation of intimate (1.5ft), personal (4ft), social (12ft) and public (25ft) spaces in his
    “Proxemics” diagrams, the artworks are an interpretation for this current time – this time of the COVID-19 Pandemic.

    ESCAPE.EXE

    “ESCAPE.EXE” is a Virtual Reality (VR) experience about escape rooms.
    Designed to implement interesting and innovative mechanics that are not possible in real life.

    EMBODIMENT

    “Embodiment” experience where players feel a strong sense of being their avatar using Virtual Reality.
    The game was a research tool to explore how embodying a different body within a game can lead to changes in emotion, movement and thought.
    This game was also a researh tool used to cure phantom pain.

    PROJECT D

    “Project D” is a racing game that is inspired by a specific genre of art, Retro wave Aesthetic.
